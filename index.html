Our Story

Located in Downtown Nowheresville, Spice is not only a Brewery and Pub, but a pretty fun place to hangout too. We feature 60 Craft Beers on tap including our house beers, an array of specialty cocktails, a full menu of upscale pub food, outdoor seating, and rooftop lounge with views of Downtown Nowheresville and Nowheresville Bay! And we're pet friendly too!
